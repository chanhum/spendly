Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your email address and a password (stored as a secure hash). When you use Spendly, we collect the expense data you enter: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly enter into the app.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and filtering by date or category. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on our servers and is associated with your account. We take reasonable precautions to protect your data, including encrypted passwords and secure server access.

You may delete your account at any time, which will permanently remove all data associated with it.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third parties. We use Google Fonts to load typefaces — this request is made by your browser directly to Google and is subject to Google's own privacy policy.

No analytics platforms, advertising networks, or data brokers are used.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact information provided on our site. We will respond as promptly as possible.
